Privacy Policy for Bar & QR Code Scanner

Effective Date: November 9, 2025

Bar & QR Code Scanner ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share while using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Bar & QR Code Scanner does not collect any personal information from users. The app only accesses data that you voluntarily scan using QR codes or barcodes. All scanned data is stored locally on your device in a secure SQL database and is not transmitted to any server or third-party.

The scanned QR codes and barcodes stored in the app are used solely for your personal use within the application. We do not share, sell, or distribute this information to any third parties.

3. Local Storage

All data scanned through Bar & QR Code Scanner is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, all stored data will be deleted.

Bar & QR Code Scanner does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information.

5. Permissions
